Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Noun Phrases as Subjects
• Structure: Use a long phrase as the subject.
• The reason why I came is a secret.
• Understanding modern art can be difficult.
名词短语作主语：使用长短语作为句子的主语，使表达更正式、更学术。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Chunking Long Subjects
Pause slightly after the long subject before saying the verb.
• The reason why I came (pause) is...
长主语的断句：在长主语和谓语动词之间稍作停顿。

Idiom 1: A matter of taste (个人喜好问题)
Explanation: 萝卜白菜各有所爱，取决于个人喜好。
Example: I didn't like the modern art exhibition, but art is always a matter of taste.

Explanation: 为某事的发生做好准备，打下基础。
Example: The invention of the internet set the stage for modern social media.

Section 4. Warm-Up Questions (10 mins)

  1. (Section 5 Question) Why is contemporary abstract art often controversial?
  2. Is judging art purely subjective or superficial? (Recycled)
  3. Does good art always evoke strong emotions?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

It is often said that art is subjective. Actually, abstract paintings confuse many people. Understanding the artist's message requires a profound mind. Some critics focus only on the aesthetic value. However, contemporary art is about ideas. The way colors are used can evoke deep sadness or joy. Some say modern art is superficial and driven by consumerism. But beauty is a matter of taste. Great art sets the stage for new ways of thinking. In my opinion, being subjective is what makes art interesting.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

It is undeniable that contemporary art divides opinion. Actually, abstract pieces can look simple. The reason why people pay millions is often linked to consumerism rather than talent. However, art should evoke feelings. Creating something unique sets a profound aesthetic standard. Some critiques are superficial. Whether you like it is a matter of taste. I believe daring artists set the stage for future innovation. To sum up, art is subjective.
