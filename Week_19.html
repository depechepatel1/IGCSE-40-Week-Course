Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Reported Speech
• Direct: "I am sick," she said.
• Reported: She said (that) she was sick. (Backshift tense: Present -> Past).
• Commands: "Eat healthy." -> He told me to eat healthy.
间接引语：引述别人的话时，时态通常要后退一步 (一般现在时 -> 一般过去时)。命令句用 'told me to...'。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Vowel shifting in reported speech
Notice how the vowel sound changes when the tense changes.
• Can /kæn/ -> Could /kʊd/
• Will /wɪl/ -> Would /wʊd/
间接引语中的元音变化：注意时态变化时元音发音的改变。

Recycled Words: (Included in list above)
Idiom 1: Under the weather (身体不适 / 抱恙)
Explanation: 觉得身体不舒服，生小病。
Example: I won't go to the gym today because I am feeling a bit under the weather.
Idiom 2: Fit as a fiddle (健壮如牛 / 身体硬朗)
Explanation: 身体非常健康，精神饱满。
Example: My grandfather is 80 years old, but he exercises every day and is fit as a fiddle.

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, health is wealth. Last month, I felt a bit under the weather. Actually, my symptoms included a headache and fever. The doctor diagnosed me with the flu and said that I needed rest. He also told me to drink plenty of water. Personally, I believe in preventive measures like exercise to boost immunity. My coach said that daily therapy would help me recover faster. His words really inspired me to take better care of myself. Now, I feel fit as a fiddle. Also, reading about health captivated my interest.

Follow-up Questions:

  • (Easy) What do you do to stay healthy?
  • (Hard) What are the biggest health challenges for people living in modern cities?

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, I remember when I caught a bad cold last winter. Actually, my main symptom was a terrible cough. To be honest, I felt completely under the weather. My mom said that I had weak immunity because I didn't eat enough vegetables. The doctor diagnosed a throat infection. He told me that I needed to take some medicine to recover fully. After a week of rest and preventive care, I was fit as a fiddle again. The experience inspired me to exercise more. Also, learning about the body captivated me.
