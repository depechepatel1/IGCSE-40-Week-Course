Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Present Perfect Continuous
• Structure: Have/Has + been + V-ing.
• Use: Actions that started in the past and continue to now, or have just finished with a result.
• I have been running for 20 minutes.
现在完成进行时：Have/Has + been + V-ing。表示从过去持续到现在的动作，或刚刚结束且有结果的动作。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Rhythm and Pacing in Lists
When listing items, go up in pitch for the first items and down on the last item.
• I like football (d), basketball (d), and tennis (e).
列举时的语调：前面几项语调上扬，最后一项语调下降。

Idiom 1: Get the ball rolling (开个好头 / 行动起来)
Explanation: 开始做某事，使事情运转或进行起来。
Example: Let's get the ball rolling and start our football training session.
Idiom 2: Keep in shape (保持身材 / 强身健体)
Explanation: 保持良好的体形和身体健康状态。
Example: I go swimming twice a week to keep in shape.

Section 4. Warm-Up Questions (10 mins)

  1. (Section 5 Question) Have you watched a tournament recently?
  2. Is buying sports equipment a big expense for you? (Recycled)
  3. Do you prefer to be a player or a spectator?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, I love sports. Recently, I have been watching the World Cup tournament on TV. Actually, the matches are very competitive. I have been trying to keep in shape, so I do a workout every morning. However, I don't have enough stamina to run a marathon. Sometimes, the referee makes bad decisions, which makes the spectators angry. Although sports gear is a big expense, I have budgeted for a new pair of running shoes. Let's get the ball rolling and go for a run now!

Follow-up Questions:

  • (Easy) What is your favorite sport to play or watch?
  • (Hard) Why are sports important for young people?

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, I recently watched a basketball match at our school. Actually, our team was playing against a rival school. To be honest, the game was extremely competitive until the very end. The players have been training for months, so their stamina was incredible. Also, the spectators were cheering loudly for every point. Although the ticket was a small expense, it was worth every penny of my budget. In the end, our team won, and we decided to get the ball rolling on a celebration party. To sum up, it was an unforgettable workout for my voice!
