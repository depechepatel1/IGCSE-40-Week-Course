QTS Aligned Teacher Lesson Plan

🎯 Learning Objectives

WALT (We Are Learning To): Discuss free time routines and active hobbies fluently. Shift away from Band 5.0 hesitation by using adverbs of frequency to describe habits naturally.

✅ Success Criteria

WILF (What I'm Looking For): 1. Exactly ONE yellow complex sentence.

2. At least TWO blue sentence starters.

3. Accurate use of the bold weekly vocabulary.

🛟 Support (Band 5.0 Target)

Provide sentence frames on the board if weaker students freeze during the speaking circuit: "My favorite pastime is ____ because..." and "I usually participate in ____." Allow them to rely on their Chinese translation table.

🚀 Stretch (Band 6.0+ Target)

Demand exact execution of the complex sentence without reading. Direct fast-finishers to the Part 3 Differentiation Stretch question (Section 10) to force spontaneous, unscripted reasoning.

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Adverbs of Frequency & Gerunds
• Adverbs: Place them before the main verb but after the 'be' verb (e.g., I usually play... / I am always happy...).
副词：放在实义动词之前，但在be动词之后 (例如: I usually play... / I am always happy...)
• Gerunds: Verbs expressing preference (enjoy, prefer, love) are followed by a V-ing gerund (e.g., I enjoy playing badminton...).
动名词：表达喜好的动词 (enjoy, prefer, love) 后面接 V-ing 形式 (例如: I enjoy playing badminton...)

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Connected Speech (Linking Consonant to Vowel)
To sound like a native speaker, do not pause between words if one ends in a consonant sound and the next begins with a vowel sound. Slide them together!
为了听起来像母语者，如果一个单词以辅音结尾，下一个单词以元音开头，请不要停顿。把它们连起来读！
• Say: have‿a blast (Sounds like: ha - va - blast)
• Say: couch‿potato (Smoothly connect the ch into the p)

Recycled Words: (None for Week 1)
Idiom 1: Couch potato (电视迷 / 懒骨头)
Explanation: 指喜欢整天坐在沙发上看电视、不爱运动的人。
Example: On weekends, I become a couch potato and watch TV shows all day.
Idiom 2: Have a blast (尽情欢乐 / 玩得痛快)
Explanation: 玩得非常开心，度过了一段极好的时光。
Example: We went to the music festival last night and had a blast.

Section 4. Warm-Up Questions (10 mins)

  1. (Section 5 Question) What activities did you usually do to relax after a long day at middle school?
  2. Do you prefer spending your free time alone or hanging out with friends?
  3. Have your hobbies changed since you started Grade 10?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, finding free time is hard for Grade 10 students. Nowadays, our schedules are very busy with heavy homework loads. However, it is still very important to unwind. Usually, many of my classmates act like a couch potato on weekends. They just watch videos online and sleep all day. Personally, I prefer active recreation. For example, I am a huge basketball enthusiast, so I always participate in school matches. When I play sports with my friends, we always have a blast. Overall, staying active is a truly fascinating pastime.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, my favorite pastime is playing badminton. Normally, I play it every Saturday afternoon at the local sports center. Actually, I rarely go alone, so I always participate with my best friends. To be honest, I used to be a couch potato before high school, but now, I am a huge sports enthusiast. Because high school is very stressful, I always get some exercise to unwind. In fact, running around the court is a fascinating form of recreation. To sum up, we highly enjoy this activity, and we always have a blast.
