Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Passive Voice (Present & Past)
• Present Passive: am/is/are + V3 (e.g., The trash is collected daily).
• Past Passive: was/were + V3 (e.g., The school was built in 1990).
被动语态：强调动作的承受者。现在时 (is done), 过去时 (was done)。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Stress on Main Verb
In passive sentences, stress the main verb (V3), not the 'be' verb.
• The work was finished on time. (Stress 'finished', not 'was')
被动语态的重音：重读主要动词 (过去分词)，不要重读 be 动词。

Idiom 1: Lend a helping hand (伸出援手)
Explanation: 伸出援手，给予他人帮助。
Example: I always try to lend a helping hand when my neighbors need it.
Idiom 2: Make a difference (发挥作用 / 产生影响)
Explanation: 产生积极的影响，带来改变。
Example: Volunteering at the animal shelter every weekend really makes a difference.

  2. Do you think it is easy for a sociable person to work in a team? (Recycled)
  3. What is a charity you know about?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, volunteering is very important. Last month, a charity event was organized by our school. Actually, we wanted to make a difference in our community. Many students were encouraged to volunteer, and a lot of money was collected for the poor. Personally, I think it is a meaningful activity. However, there was a small conflict about the schedule. Fortunately, we managed to compromise and work together. In my opinion, being sociable helps when you want to contribute to a team. If everyone tries to lend a helping hand, a huge positive impact can be made.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, I once helped my neighbor organize a charity sale. Actually, the event was held in our local community center. To be honest, I wanted to contribute because I believe it is a meaningful cause. The clothes were donated by many families, and they were sold at a low price. At first, my neighbor and I had different ideas, but we decided to compromise. Because I am quite sociable, I enjoyed talking to the customers. A significant impact was made because we raised a lot of money to lend a helping hand to those in need. To sum up, I was proud to be a volunteer.
