Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Hedging (Cautious Language)
• Structure: It could be argued / It is possible that / Some might say.
• Use: To soften opinions and sound more academic/objective.
• It could be argued that technology makes us lazy.
模糊限制语：用于委婉、客观地表达观点，避免过于绝对。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Intonation for Hedging
Use a rise-fall intonation to sound thoughtful and open to debate.
• It ^could be argued...
语调：使用升降调，听起来更深思熟虑且乐于讨论。

Idiom 1: Let one's hair down (放松束缚 / 尽情狂欢)
Explanation: 彻底放松，不拘小节，尽情享受。

Example: I like to spend time in nature to recharge my batteries.

Section 4. Warm-Up Questions (10 mins)

  1. (Section 5 Question) How has technology changed societal relaxation?
  2. Do you think a sedentary lifestyle is inevitable for office workers? (Recycled)
  3. What activities do you pursue to feel invigorated?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

It could be argued that modern life is too fast. Nowadays, many people have a sedentary lifestyle. Actually, they spend hours sitting in front of screens. Some experts predict that this will have inevitable health consequences. However, it is important to pursue hobbies that stimulate the mind and body. For example, hiking can be very invigorateing. Others might say that solitude is therapeutic for mental health. Personally, I need to let my hair down on weekends. It allows me to recharge my batteries for the week ahead.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

It is often said that technology has transformed leisure. In the past, people used to enjoy active games. To be honest, today's youth are more sedentary. It could be argued that digital entertainment fails to invigorate us like physical exercise does. However, online games can stimulate cognitive skills. While some predict a decline in social skills, others find solitude in gaming therapeutic. It seems inevitable that we will rely more on screens. Nevertheless, we must find ways to recharge our batteries offline. To sum up, balance is key.
