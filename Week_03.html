QTS Aligned Teacher Lesson Plan

🎯 Learning Objectives

WALT (We Are Learning To): Describe memorable meals and eating habits using specific vocabulary. Use the Present Perfect Tense to talk about life experiences with food.

✅ Success Criteria

WILF (What I'm Looking For): 1. Exactly ONE yellow complex sentence.

2. At least TWO blue sentence starters.

3. Accurate use of the bold weekly vocabulary.

🛟 Support (Band 5.0 Target)

Sentence frames: "My favorite meal is ____ because..." and "I have never eaten ____." rely on Chinese translation table.

🚀 Stretch (Band 6.0+ Target)

Use sensory adjectives (spicy, bitter, crunchy) to describe food in detail. Direct fast-finishers to Section 10.

Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Quantifiers & Present Perfect
• Quantifiers: Use too much (uncountable) and too many (countable). (e.g., I ate too much cake / too many cookies).
量词：too much + 不可数名词; too many + 可数名词
• Present Perfect: Use have/has + V3 for experiences. (e.g., I have tried sushi before.).
现在完成时：have/has + 过去分词，表示经历

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Long Vowel Sounds
Stretch out the vowel sounds in words like food (fuuuud), eat (eeet), and meal (meeeal). Do not cut them short!
长元音：拉长元音发音，不要读得太短促！

Recycled Words: (Included in list above)
Idiom 1: Have a sweet tooth (嗜甜如命 / 喜食甜食)
Explanation: 极其喜欢吃糖果、甜点等甜食。
Example: I have a sweet tooth, so I always order chocolate cake after dinner.
Idiom 2: A piece of cake (小菜一碟 / 易如反掌)
Explanation: 形容做某件事情非常容易，毫不费力。
Example: For a professional chef, cooking a big family dinner is a piece of cake.

  2. Do you prefer homemade food or eating out at restaurants?
  3. Have you ever tried to cook a nutritious meal by yourself?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, I have always had a huge appetite. Recently, I watched an informative documentary about sugar, and I learned that it is very addictive. However, I have a sweet tooth, so it is hard for me to stop eating cakes. Usually, I try to consume healthy ingredients like vegetables and fish. Although fast food is tasty, I know that homemade food is much more nutritious. Actually, I have tried Japanese cuisine many times, and I love it. Overall, cooking healthy food is not a piece of cake, but it is worth it.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, the most memorable meal I have ever had was a dinner at my grandmother's house. Normally, we visit her every Spring Festival. Actually, she is an amazing cook, and her homemade dumplings are delicious. To be honest, the ingredients she uses are always fresh from her garden. Because the food was so nutritious and tasty, I consumed three huge bowls! In fact, I have such a big appetite when I am there. To sum up, traditional Chinese cuisine is my favorite, and eating with family is never a piece of cake to forget. Also, learning about her recipes was very informative and the taste was truly addictive.
