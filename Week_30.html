Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Cleft Sentences
• What-cleft: What I need is a hug. (Focus on 'a hug').
• It-cleft: It was John who called. (Focus on 'John').
强调句：What... is... / It is... that...。用于强调句子的某一部分。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Emphasis in Cleft Sentences
Stress the information that follows the verb 'to be'.
• What I need is a HUG.
• It was JOHN who called.
强调句的重音：重读 be 动词后面的强调内容。

Recycled Words: (Included in list above)
Idiom 1: Through thick and thin (同甘共苦 / 风雨同舟)
Explanation: 在任何情况下，不顾艰难险阻，同甘共苦。
Example: True friends are the ones who stay by your side through thick and thin.
Idiom 2: See the best in someone (看到别人的闪光点)
Explanation: 专注于发现某人性格或行为中好的一面。
Example: My mother is a very kind woman who always tries to see the best in everyone.

Section 4. Warm-Up Questions (10 mins)

  1. (Section 5 Question) How has digital communication affected human empathy?
  2. Should we endorse people who are superficial? (Recycled)
  3. How do you nurture a harmonious relationship?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

It seems that technology connects us. However, screens can alienate people. Actually, online chats are often superficial. What we need is more empathy towards others, especially those who are vulnerable. Some people hide behind avatars. To build a harmonious society, we must nurture real connections. It is trust that allows relationships to persevere through hard times. I don't endorse cyberbullying. True friends stick together through thick and thin. We should always see the best in someone.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

It is clear that digital communication is fast. However, what worries me is the lack of empathy. Actually, texts can be superficial and alienate friends. It is face-to-face contact that helps us nurture harmonious bonds. Without it, we feel vulnerable. Although technology is useful, what matters most is that we persevere in meeting people. I endorse putting phones away. To sum up, real feelings need real presence.
