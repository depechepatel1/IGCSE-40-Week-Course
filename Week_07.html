WALT (We Are Learning To): Discuss future careers and job requirements. Use Modal Verbs of Obligation to describe duties and rules.

✅ Success Criteria

WILF (What I'm Looking For): 1. Exactly TWO yellow complex sentences.

2. At least TWO blue sentence starters.

3. Accurate use of the bold weekly vocabulary.

🛟 Support (Band 5.0 Target)

Sentence frames: "I want to be a..." and "A doctor must..."

Use formal vocabulary to describe job responsibilities and career paths.

Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Modal Verbs of Obligation
• Must: Strong internal obligation (I must study).
• Have to: External obligation/rule (I have to wear a uniform).
• Should: Advice (You should sleep early).
情态动词：Must (主观义务), Have to (客观规定), Should (建议)。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Silent Letters
Be careful with silent letters! Do not pronounce the letters in brackets:
• Colleag(u)e
• Resig(n)
• Wri(t)e
不发音字母：注意单词中不发音的字母，如 Colleague 中的 ue，Resign 中的 g。

Recycled Words: (Included in list above)
Idiom 1: Learn the ropes (摸清门道 / 熟悉业务)
Explanation: 学习如何做某项工作或掌握基本窍门。
Example: It took me a few weeks to learn the ropes at my new part-time job.
Idiom 2: Climb the corporate ladder (步步高升 / 职场晋阶)
Explanation: 在公司或职场中逐步晋升到更高的职位。
Example: He works very hard every day because he wants to climb the corporate ladder.

Section 4. Warm-Up Questions (10 mins)

  1. (Section 5 Question) What profession would you like to have in the future?
  2. Do you need a high qualification to be a doctor? (Recycled)
  3. Are you an ambitious person?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, choosing a future profession is difficult. In my opinion, being a doctor is a noble job, but you have to study for many years. Also, you must get a high qualification from a university. If you are motivated and hardworking, you can become a successful candidate for the job. However, some people just want to climb the corporate ladder and get a promotion quickly. Personally, I am ambitious, but I also want to help people. Ideally, I would like to work with a friendly colleague who can help me learn the ropes. Finally, I would never resign if I loved my job.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, I would love to be a software engineer. Actually, this profession is very popular nowadays. To be honest, you have to be very smart and patient to write code. Because technology is changing fast, you must obtain a high qualification in Computer Science. Also, I am very motivated to create new apps. In the future, I hope to work with a brilliant colleague at a big tech company. If I work hard, I might get a promotion and become a manager. To sum up, I am very ambitious about this career path.
