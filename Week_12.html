WALT (We Are Learning To): Talk about personal achievements and challenges. Use the Past Perfect to describe events before a past time.

Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Past Perfect
• Structure: Had + V3.
• Use: For an action that happened before another past action.
• When I arrived, the train had left.
过去完成时：Had + 过去分词。表示“过去的过去”，即在过去某个动作之前已经发生的动作。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Dropping the 'h'
In rapid speech, 'had' often sounds like 'd. Drop the 'h'.
• I'd (I had) finished.
• She'd (She had) left.
省音：快速语流中，had 常读作 'd，省略 h 音。

Recycled Words: (Included in list above)
Idiom 1: Pass with flying colors (大获全胜 / 成绩斐然)
Explanation: 以优异的成绩通过考试或完成任务。
Example: She studied very hard and passed her English exam with flying colors.
Idiom 2: Pay off (终得回报 / 功夫不负有心人)
Explanation: 付出的努力最终得到好结果或回报。
Example: All those late nights of studying finally paid off when I got into a top university.

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, learning English was hard for me. Before I came to this school, I had never spoken to a foreigner. Actually, I had to overcome many obstacles. I had dedicated hours to studying vocabulary before I finally saw an improvement. Eventually, my hard work paid off. Also, my teacher's help had contributed greatly to my progress. By the time I took the exam, I had accomplished my goal of learning 500 words. It was a big milestone when I passed with flying colors. In my opinion, this achievement had a huge impact on my confidence to fulfill my dreams.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, a difficult task I succeeded in was learning to swim. Actually, I was terrified of water. Before I started lessons, I had never entered a pool. Although I was scared, I had dedicated my summer to overcome this fear. To be honest, the biggest obstacle was holding my breath. After I had accomplished swimming one lap, I felt like I had fulfilled a big dream. My coach's advice had contributed a lot to my success. In the end, my efforts paid off and I passed with flying colors in the final test. This milestone had a lasting impact on me.
