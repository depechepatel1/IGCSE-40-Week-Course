Student Handout

Section 1. Weekly Grammar Point (5 mins)

Causative Verbs (Have/Get something done)
• Structure: Have/Get + Object + V3.
• Meaning: You pay/ask someone else to do it for you.
• I had my hair cut. (I didn't cut it myself).
使役动词：Have/Get + 宾语 + 过去分词。表示“请/让别人做某事” (例如: I had my hair cut = 我去理发了)。

Section 2. Weekly Pronunciation Point (3 mins)

Syllable Counting
Clap the syllables in long words to say them clearly.
• Com-for-ta-ble (4)
• Acc-ess-or-ies (4)

Recycled Words: (Included in list above)
Idiom 1: Dress to kill (盛装打扮 / 惊艳全场)
Explanation: 穿着极其迷人、引人注目。
Example: She walked into the party dressed to kill in a beautiful red gown.
Idiom 2: Shop till you drop (血拼到底)
Explanation: 疯狂购物，直到累得走不动。
Example: During the winter sales, my friends and I usually shop till we drop.

Section 4. Warm-Up Questions (10 mins)

  1. (Section 5 Question) Do you prefer buying trendy clothes or vintage ones?
  2. Is shopping in a mall too hectic for you? (Recycled)
  3. What is your favorite clothing brand?

Section 5. Shadowing text adapted from the Cambridge IGCSE ESL Coursebook (3 mins)

To be honest, I love fashion. Yesterday, I went to the mall to browse some shops. Actually, I wanted to buy a trendy new garment for a party. Because I wanted to look perfect, I had my hair done at a salon. However, designer brands are not always affordable. Sometimes, I prefer vintage clothes because they are unique. Shopping can be hectic, but finding a tranquil cafe to rest in helps me relax. Tomorrow, I plan to dress to kill. My friends and I will probably shop till we drop.

Section 7. Model Answer (5 mins)

Well, my favorite item is a black leather jacket. Actually, it is a vintage piece I bought in London. To be honest, it was quite affordable for such a cool garment. I usually get it cleaned professionally to keep it looking new. Also, it is very trendy and matches everything. Even when my life is hectic, wearing this jacket makes me feel calm and tranquil. I love to wear it when I browse the city streets. To sum up, it helps me dress to kill whenever I go out.
